### What I Practiced / Built
- Worked on project: AgentArena — Multi-Agent Debate & Consensus System  

  **Concept:**
  - Instead of relying on a single LLM response, multiple agents with different personas debate and produce a more reliable answer  

  **System Design:**
  - 5 agents with distinct roles:
    - Skeptic  
    - Optimist  
    - Domain Expert  
    - Devil’s Advocate  
    - Pragmatist  

  **Workflow:**
  - Round 1: Each agent gives an independent argument  
  - Round 2: Agents review others’ responses and provide rebuttals  
  - Round 3: Final positions are submitted  
  - Judge Agent synthesizes a final consensus, highlighting agreements and disagreements  

  **Output:**
  - Full debate transcript  
  - Final verdict generated by Judge Agent  
  - UI displays both side by side
